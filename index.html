How to Use
The optimizer searches for the fighter build that clears your target dungeon level for the lowest gold cost.
  • Your fighters must already be placed in the Battle Formation grid on the Dungeon tab in this order:
    Shadow Dancer
    Bastion
    Berserker
    Crusader
    Paladin
    Priest
  • Choose the dungeon level you want to optimize against. It's advised not to aim too far ahead of your current progress, as some levels may simply not be optimized given the current constraints. Conversely, if the target level is one you can already clear with a consistent win rate, no optimization is applied.
  • Turning on "Use zeroed fighters" lets the optimizer search from a blank slate instead of your fighters' current stats. This might find a better distribution, but takes longer to compute.
  • In the current state of the game, stats impact is marginal compared to fighter items, so stat optimization may only produce marginal gains.
